The Awakening of Tanemahuta


A day in the life of a tree is a simple one, feeling the breeze between your leaves and listening to the sounds of the Kereru and all manner of creatures crawling through the dense Ngahere. The tall Kauri lived happily amongst his little friends until one day a strange and tiny two legged creature attempted to chip away at the thick bark at the base of his heavy trunk. The creature wore funny bright coloured cloth over his body and carried a shinny silver tool. The curious Kauri must speak to the strange little animal
